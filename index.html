03 About

Sebastian van Eickelen is a filmmaker and photographer based in Geneva, working across documentary, editorial, and commercial projects. His practice combines cinematic image-making with a restrained, structured approach shaped by precision, atmosphere, and narrative clarity.

Available for campaigns, editorial commissions, and long-form documentary work.
