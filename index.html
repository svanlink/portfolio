03 About
Filmmaker · Photographer · Director

Sebastian van Eickelen is a filmmaker and photographer based in Geneva, working across documentary, editorial, and commercial projects. His practice combines cinematic image-making with a restrained, structured approach shaped by precision, atmosphere, and narrative clarity.

Available for campaigns, editorial commissions, and long-form documentary work.

04 Services
01

Creative Direction

Concept, treatment, and visual direction from first frame to final cut.

From first brief to final treatment: visual concept, references, casting logic, location tone, and the authored approach that holds the whole project together.

02

Direction

Brand films, documentary portraits, and campaign narratives with clear authorship.

Performance, pacing, and image logic shaped on set with the final edit already in mind. Work that reads directed rather than merely captured.

03

Cinematography

Camera, lenses, light, and movement handled with a disciplined visual system.

Lenses chosen for the project, not the spec sheet. Light handled practically and cinematically. Frames built to hold atmosphere, precision, and narrative weight.

04

Photography

Editorial and commercial stills with the same tonal discipline as the motion work.

Portrait, product, and editorial stills made with the same authored eye. Technically rigorous, atmospherically considered, and consistent with the moving-image work when both live in the same project.

05

Editorial Post

Structure, pacing, sound, and finishing shaped into a coherent final piece.

Offline and online edit in DaVinci Resolve, with sound design and transitions judged against the register of the image. No templates. Every cut is a decision.

06

Color Finishing

Final grade and tonal continuity calibrated to medium, story, and delivery.

Tonal range calibrated to the medium and the story. Deliverables in any format required. Every frame holds the same register as the one before it.
